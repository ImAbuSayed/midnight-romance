[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I see the midnight melting in your eyes
So let the shadows sever all our ties
I want to pull you into something deep
Where all the secrets of the night we keep

[Verse 1]
The gravel roads are miles and miles away
But right here in the dark is where I stay
You have a smile that sets the room alight
A gentle spark that burns away the night

[Pre-Chorus]
I feel the beating of your heart against my skin
A silent storm is ready to begin
We leave the noise and all the rush behind
You put a sweeter kind of trouble on my mind

[Chorus]
Let me hold you where the moonlight falls
Breaking down the borders and the walls
Just you and me beneath a painted sky
A midnight romance that will never die

[Post-Chorus]
This midnight romance burning in the dark
A slow surrender from a single spark

[Verse 2]
Your fingers tracing circles on my hand
A quiet language I can understand
I catch the heavy warmth upon your breath
A tender moment I could love to death

[Pre-Chorus 2]
I feel the beating of your heart against my skin
A silent storm is ready to begin
We leave the noise and all the rush behind
You put a sweeter kind of trouble on my mind

[Chorus]
Let me hold you where the moonlight falls
Breaking down the borders and the walls
Just you and me beneath a painted sky
A midnight romance that will never die

[Post-Chorus 2]
This midnight romance burning in the dark
A slow surrender from a single spark

[Bridge]
The shadows fade when holding something true
A quiet world leaves only what we knew
There is a fire hidden in the deep
A promise made that we are bound to keep
And Saeed sings the quiet of the flame
A whispered echo calling out a name

[Spoken]
You know there is nothing else but this right now
Just let the silence take control somehow

[Final Chorus]
Let me hold you where the moonlight falls
Breaking down the borders and the walls
Just you and me beneath a painted sky
A midnight romance that will never die
